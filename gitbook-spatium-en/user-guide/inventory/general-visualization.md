# General Visualization

The inventory includes a primary level of data reflecting parking behavior. This information is available on its homepage to summarize the status of all your locations.

The capacity of each location is also displayed in real time based on its individual capacity, showing the number of available spots (in green) and occupied spots (in red) for each of its zones.

Additionally, by hovering over a zone, you can view the occupancy distribution by type of spot within each zone. For more details, click on the “Distribution” icon, which will open a floating menu labeled “Spots Distribution”.

The types of spots displayed automatically adjust based on those available in the specific zone.

The same data, along with additional metrics like average usage time and average daily or weekly turnover, are available when entering a location. You can switch between these options using the 'Metrics' button on the right.
