# REPORTS

The “Reports” section of the platform allows you to view and analyze data with flexibility, enabling you to assess parking behavior and build business intelligence. In this section, you can create as many distinct reports as you wish, and these reports will be specific to your account to avoid potential conflicts.

<p align="center"><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown.png" alt=""></p>

By clicking on the “Reports” section in Spatium's main menu, you will be redirected to your dedicated reporting space on the “Grafana” platform.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (72).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You will then be able to find all the reports you have created based on your needs: from simple occupancy reports to revenue reports (when your PARCS system is integrated with Spatium), as well as advanced business intelligence reports that combine revenue, discounts, and occupancy to provide more context and advice for optimizing your performance.

In each report, you can:

* Select the relevant data for you using various filters;
* Move different widgets for a layout that suits you;
* Change their types of visualizations;
* Export the data.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (73).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



The various filters that allow you to select the appropriate data are located at the top of the page, with the date selector on the top right. You can also change the position of your widgets by selecting their title and dragging them. You can adjust their size by grabbing the bottom right corner. Additionally, you can export the data by clicking on the widget title, selecting “Inspect / Data” and then “Download CSV”.

Finally, you can share the entire report by clicking the share icon to the right of its title at the top of the page.

If you need more technical modifications or additions, contact our support team or your sales representative so that we can assist you as quickly as possible.
