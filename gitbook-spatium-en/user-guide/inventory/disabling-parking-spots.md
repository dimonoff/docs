# Disabling Parking Spots

The number of available and occupied spots displayed on the platform for a location, a zone, or a group only considers active spots with a behavior defined as “automatic” by default. However, it is possible to disable a parking spot to exclude its status from these counters, which can be useful for special events, construction work, etc.

To do this, select the spot(s) to deactivate and toggle the “Disable Spots” option in the floating menu that appears on the left side of the screen. This action is only available to project administrators. If you do not have access to this option, consult your administrator.

Once the spot is disabled, its real status will still be represented by the color of its icon on the layout to continue indicating its status. However, the spot will no longer be counted in the group, zone, or spot counters, nor on the dynamic display panels. Additionally, a disabled indicator will appear in the top-right corner of its icon.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (51).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



To reactivate one or more spots, simply select them again and reverse the process. Their status will then be counted in real time once again.
