# Zone Creation

Just like when creating your first location, the option to create your first zone will appear in the floating menu on the map. A message will indicate this, along with a button to create your first zone.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (23).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

By clicking on the “Add a Zone to This Location” button (highlighted in red in the previous image), the “New Zone” floating menu will appear, allowing you to enter its name, zone type, and layout to display it on the map. Once the information is entered, click the “Submit” button.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (24).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



Once submitted, the zone will appear in the floating menu specific to the location it is in. You can click on it in the menu to view detailed information, such as its real-time parking data.

This step is described in more detail in the section "Create Locations and Zones" in the “Inventory” chapter.
