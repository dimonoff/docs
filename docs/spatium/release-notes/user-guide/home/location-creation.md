# Location Creation

If this is your first login and the project has not been set up in advance, there may be no locations available on the platform. In that case, the floating menu on the map will display a message informing you of this, along with an option to create your first location.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (20).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

By clicking on the “Create a New Location” button (highlighted in red in the previous image), the “Create a New Location” floating menu will appear, allowing you to enter its name and address to display it on the map. Once the information is entered, click the “Submit” button.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (21).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Once submitted, the location will appear in the floating menu on the map. You can click on it either on the map or in the menu to zoom in and view detailed information such as its zones or real-time parking data.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (22).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



This step is described in more detail in the section "Create Locations and Zones" in the “Inventory" chapter.
