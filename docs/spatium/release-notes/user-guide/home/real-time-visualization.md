# Real-Time Visualization

At any time and in real time, it is possible from the floating map menu in the home menu to view all of your locations at a glance, as well as their availability and occupancy data.

This floating menu can be reduced in order to have a better display of the map by clicking on the upper left icon (see red boxes below).

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (25).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Each location will then display the number of occupied spots in red and the number of available spots in green. Below the name of each location, there is also a progress bar representing the parking capacity based on the total number of spots.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (26).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



You can also see a blinking blue dot to the left of this progress bar, indicating whether the data from the different locations is up-to-date or if an issue has occurred, causing a delay in the data display (see the image below). In this case, the dot turns gray, and a message indicating the time of the last update appears when you hover over it with the mouse.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (27).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



To view the data of a specific location, you can click on it in the floating menu or directly on the map. Spatium will then zoom in and display the location's information and data.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (28).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You can view more details about the location, such as the number of spots with an unknown status, indicating a potential problem, as well as performance data for the day, such as the average occupancy time per hour or the average turnover rate for the location.

Each zone within the location will also become visible, showing their occupancy and availability data, just like the locations.

You can also enter a location or zone from this menu by clicking on “Visit the location” (highlighted in red in the image above) or “→” (highlighted in blue in the image above), respectively. To make the “Open” button for a zone appear, hover over its availability data in red and green on its right.

The floating menu is consistent throughout the platform. Zone data is detailed in the section “Indoor Zone Data”.
