# Services

After selecting the relevant parking spots in edit mode, you can configure a type for your parking spots and assign services such as valet, car wash, etc. These services allow for specific management of these spots with particular behaviors and can be used, for example, in reports.\
To do this, go to the 'Services' tab in the floating menu after selecting the concerned spots in edit mode\[AO1] \[ct2] . You can select the services to add. It is possible to add services that are not yet available on the platform by contacting our support. Once the service(s) are selected, click “Apply”, otherwise the modification will not be saved, then click “Save” to exit edit mode.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (70).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You will then see a new icon representing the service on the plan and can filter these spots according to their services using the filters in the “Spots” tab.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (71).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Note: The service(s) assigned to a spot will change the appearance of the spot's icon. If a spot has no service, the icon will correspond to its type.
