# Actions

**Actions** are commands that the system can send to devices. They represent an evolution of traditional device commands, offering greater flexibility by allowing the sending of custom payloads defined by the client.

#### Accessing Actions

To access actions, follow these steps:

1. Click on **Actions** in the main menu.
2. Two tabs are available:
   * **Action List**: Displays the list of configured actions.
   * **Action Types**: Shows the different types of available actions.
